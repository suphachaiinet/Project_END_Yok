Teacher Portal Guide

Dashboard

The dashboard gives you an overview of student performance across all labs. Here you can see:

  • Total number of students enrolled
  • Average score across all labs
  • Overall completion rate
  • Most recent student activity

Lab Management

Each lab card takes you to a detailed view for that lab where you can:

  • View student submissions and scores
  • Manually adjust scores if needed
  • Edit the keywords used for automatic grading
  • Delete incorrect or test submissions

Students

From the sidebar or Quick Actions, you can access the student management page to:

  • View all enrolled students
  • See completion rates and average scores for each student
  • Reset student passwords

Manage PDFs

You can update the lab instructions for students by:

  • Selecting the lab you want to update
  • Uploading a new PDF file
  • The system will automatically make this available to students

Statistics

The statistics page provides more detailed analytics on student performance:

  • Lab-by-lab completion rates
  • Average scores for each lab
  • Identifying which labs students find most challenging

Your Account

You can manage your own account from the dropdown menu in the top-right:

  • Change your password
  • Log out of the portal

If you need additional help or have questions about using the portal, please contact the system administrator.
